From April 17 to 27, Prof. Alfred visited several prestigious universities and institutes and communicated with local professors and researchers. 

On 17 April 2026, Prof. Alfred gave a seminar at the Max Planck Institute for Heart and Lung Research at Bad Nauheim, Germany and had the precious time with Prof. Thomas Braun and Prof. Rajkumar Savai.
